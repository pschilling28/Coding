What is She told me a Story?

A monthly open mic meetup for women in the Malmö tech community and beyond to share, empower, and support each other through storytelling and in-person connections. Getting to know each other on a human level, beyond 30-second elevator pitches and elaborate LinkedIn profiles over wine, coffee, and tea (pick your poison). No titles, no cool kids’ clubs, just human connections. Come and share your story from your professional or personal life. Read a poem, tell a joke, swear, it’s up to you!

Heading Elements

Element Name Description
<h1>

Heading 1

The largest, most prominent heading. Often used for titles or major sections of a page.
<h2>

Heading 2

The next step below the first heading in terms of size and emphasis.
<h3>

Heading 3

What more to say than a smaller heading than the previous.
<h4>

Heading 4

Getting smaller still.
<h5>
Heading 5
The itsy bitsy heading went up the water spout.

Heading 6
